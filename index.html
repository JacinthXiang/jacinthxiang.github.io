KIPPER KIPPERS!

KIPPER KIPPERS is a branding and package design based on the needs of the new generation of migration of the city to experience the traditional process of making kippers.

LA DURÉE

Starting from the same point, life also has different trajectories. From birth to death, we have to go through a variety of unknowns and grow in them. Life cannot be summed up in one book. What kind of life do you want to create? This is what this book wants you to think about while reading.

SOUNDSCAPE

SOUNDSCAPE is an interactive project discussing the hierarchy of conversations among public spaces. According to the environmental attributes, the dialogue content and the sound state will be verified. We could extract and analyze the big data of words, language, and frequency in dialogues to know what people's concern changes place to place. How surrounding forms them and how they affect the environment.

BRUSH PHILOSOPHY

It is far less interesting to discuss the issue of artistic standards today. When we try our best to put aside these trivialities in life and turn life into a carrier of productivity, human and machines, human and production methods all undergo a super-type collection. Man is no longer a subject of society. Man is substituted into social production and becomes a tool of social production.
