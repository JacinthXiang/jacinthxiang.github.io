KIPPER KIPPERS!

KIPPER KIPPERS is a brand and package design based on the needs of the new generation of migration of the city to experience the traditional process of making kippers.

LA DURÉE

Starting from the same point, life has different trajectories. From birth to death, we have to go through a variety of unknowns and grow in them. Life cannot be summed up in one book. What kind of life do you want to create? This is what this book wants you to think about while reading.

SOUNDSCAPE

SOUNDSCAPE is an interactive project discussing the hierarchy of conversations among public spaces. I extract and technically analyze the big data of words, language, and frequency of words in dialogues, to figure out: What people's concern changes from place to place? How surrounding forms them and how they affect the environment?

BRUSH PHILOSOPHY

When we try our best to put aside the trivialities in life and turn life into a carrier of productivity, human and machines, human and production methods all undergo a super-type collection. Man is no longer a subject of society, which is switched to be substituted into social production and becomes a tool of social production.

SLOGAN PROJECT

SLOGAN PROJECT is a visual experiment and social observation during the time of covid-19 isolation. Since I live in Wuhan, which is the first place people find infected by the coronavirus. I choose this city as a sample for slogan propaganda in modern China.
