Brush Philosophy

BRUSH PHOLOSOPHY discusses the invasion of efficiency in daily life. When we try our best to put aside the trivialities in life and turn life into a carrier of productivity, human and machines, human and production methods all undergo a super-type collection. Man is no longer a subject of society, which is switched to be substituted into social production and becomes a tool of social production.
